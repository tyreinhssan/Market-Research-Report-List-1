<p><ul><li>Single-Sided Tape</li><li>Double-Sided Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration damping tape market consists of two primary types: single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape is designed to be applied to one surface, effectively reducing vibrations by absorbing energy and minimizing resonance. In contrast, double-sided tape adheres to both surfaces, providing enhanced damping performance by isolating the vibration sources more effectively between two materials. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics to improve product durability and comfort by mitigating unwanted vibrations.</p></p>
