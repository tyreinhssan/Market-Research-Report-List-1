<p><p>The commercial coffee brewers market includes various types designed for different service environments. Satellite brewers feature a separate insulated container for serving, ideal for self-service setups. Decanter brewers brew directly into glass decanters, suitable for buffet-style settings. Airpot brewers use vacuum-sealed containers, extending coffee freshness for long periods while allowing easy dispensing. Coffee urns are large-capacity units perfect for events or gatherings, brewing substantial quantities at once, catering to high-demand situations efficiently. Each type serves specific operational needs in the foodservice industry.</p></p>
