<p><ul><li>Single Basin</li><li>Dual Basin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endoscopic reprocessors are crucial for cleaning and sterilizing endoscopes used in medical procedures. The market is divided into two main types: single basin and dual basin. Single basin reprocessors utilize one chamber for cleaning, which is efficient for smaller facilities or low to moderate volumes of endoscopic procedures. Dual basin reprocessors, however, feature separate chambers for cleaning and rinsing, allowing for enhanced throughput and efficiency, making them suitable for larger hospitals or high-volume settings requiring rigorous sterilization processes.</p></p>
