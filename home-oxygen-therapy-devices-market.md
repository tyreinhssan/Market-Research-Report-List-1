<p><ul><li>Oxygen Source Equipment</li><li>Oxygen Delivery Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home oxygen therapy devices market comprises two primary categories: oxygen source equipment and oxygen delivery devices. Oxygen source equipment includes concentrators, which extract oxygen from ambient air, and compressed gas cylinders that store high-pressure oxygen for patient use. Oxygen delivery devices facilitate the transport of oxygen to patients, featuring systems like nasal cannulas, masks, and ventilators, ensuring accurate flow rates and comfort. Together, these devices cater to patients with respiratory conditions, enhancing their quality of life at home.</p></p>
