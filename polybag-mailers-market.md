<p><p>Polybag mailers are essential packaging solutions used for shipping various items. The market is primarily categorized into two types: self-adhesive sealing and zip sealing. Self-adhesive sealing mailers feature a sticky flap that allows easy closure, providing a secure and tamper-evident seal. In contrast, zip sealing mailers utilize a zip-lock mechanism, enabling users to repeatedly open and close the bag while ensuring a tight seal. Both types offer durability and protection for contents during transit, catering to diverse shipping needs.</p></p>
