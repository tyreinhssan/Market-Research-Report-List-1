<p><p>Metal fibre burners are categorized into two primary types: Infrared Mode Type and Blue Flame Mode Type. Infrared Mode Type burners utilize metal fibers to emit radiant heat, making them efficient for high-temperature applications with minimal emissions. In contrast, Blue Flame Mode Type burners create a visible blue flame that indicates complete combustion, offering an aesthetically pleasing flame with high efficiency. Both types cater to various industrial heating needs, emphasizing energy efficiency and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
