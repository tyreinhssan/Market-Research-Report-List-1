<p><p>Negative feedback control linear regulators are essential for maintaining voltage stability in various electronic devices. The market can be categorized into two main types: series type and shunt type. Series type linear regulators control the output voltage by varying the resistance in series with the load, making them ideal for efficient power distribution. In contrast, shunt type regulators divert excess current to maintain constant voltage, suitable for applications requiring low power and precise voltage regulation. Both types cater to distinct market needs.</p></p>
