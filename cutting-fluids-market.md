<p><strong>The Cutting Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Cutting Fluids</li><li>Emulsified Cutting Fluids</li><li>Semi-synthetic Cutting Fluids</li><li>Synthetic Cutting Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting fluids market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Oil cutting fluids are pure petroleum oils, offering excellent lubrication but limited cooling. Emulsified cutting fluids combine oil with water, providing balanced cutting performance and cooling. Semi-synthetic cutting fluids blend vegetable oils and water, delivering enhanced lubrication while minimizing environmental impact. Synthetic cutting fluids contain no oil, using chemical formulations for superior cooling and lubrication, ideal for high-precision machining and reducing residue. Each type caters to different machining needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
