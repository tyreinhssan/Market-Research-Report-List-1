<p><p>The industrial film market comprises various types of polymer films tailored for specific applications. Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) offers flexibility and toughness. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is lightweight and transparent, while High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is known for its strength and resistance. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) and Bi-axially Oriented PET (BOPET) exhibit high clarity and durability. Polypropylene (PP) and its oriented form (BOPP) provide excellent moisture barrier properties. Cast Polypropylene (CPP) is valued for its gloss and softness. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is versatile and resilient. Polyamide and Bi-axially Oriented Polyamide (BOPA) films offer high tensile strength. Other types may include specialty films for niche applications.</p></p>
