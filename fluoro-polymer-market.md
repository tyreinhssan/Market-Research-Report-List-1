<p><p>The fluoropolymer market consists of several key types, including PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride), and fluoroelastomers, among others. PTFE is renowned for its exceptional chemical resistance and non-stick properties, making it ideal for various industrial applications. PVDF is valued for its high purity and mechanical strength, often used in coatings and piping. Fluoroelastomers offer flexibility and temperature resistance, suitable for seals and gaskets. The "Others" category includes specialized fluoropolymers serving niche applications, diversifying the market.</p></p>
