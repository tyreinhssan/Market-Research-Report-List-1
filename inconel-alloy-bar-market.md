<p><ul><li>Solution Strengthened Alloy</li><li>Age Hardening Alloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inconel alloy bars are categorized into two main market types: solution strengthened alloys and age hardening alloys. Solution strengthened alloys are created by solid-solution strengthening, enhancing their strength and corrosion resistance for high-temperature applications. In contrast, age hardening alloys undergo a process where heat treatment maximizes strength through precipitation of intermetallic compounds. Both types are crucial in industries like aerospace, chemical processing, and power generation due to their exceptional mechanical properties and ability to withstand extreme environments.</p></p>
