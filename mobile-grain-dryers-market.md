<p><p>The mobile grain dryers market can be categorized into three main types: mounted, self-propelled, and trailed. Mounted dryers are affixed to other farming equipment, offering ease of use and transportation. Self-propelled dryers operate independently, equipped with their own engines for mobility across fields. Trailed dryers attach to tractors, allowing for flexibility in movement while utilizing the tractor's power for operation. Each type caters to different operational needs, helping farmers efficiently manage moisture levels in harvested grain.</p></p>
