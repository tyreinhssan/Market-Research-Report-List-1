<p><strong>The Calcium Nitrate Fertilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalline</li><li>Granular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calcium nitrate fertilizer market is segmented into two primary types: crystalline and granular. Crystalline calcium nitrate is typically water-soluble and used in hydroponics and foliar applications due to its quick nutrient absorption. Granular calcium nitrate, on the other hand, offers slow-release properties, making it ideal for soil applications and long-term crop needs. Both forms provide essential calcium and nitrogen, enhancing plant growth and productivity, but their application methods and environmental interactions differ significantly.</p></p>
