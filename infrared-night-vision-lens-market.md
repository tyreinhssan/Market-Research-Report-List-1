<p><p>The infrared night vision lens market comprises two primary types: monocular and binocular lenses. Monocular infrared night vision lenses are compact, lightweight devices designed for single-eye use, making them ideal for quick, portable applications such as wildlife observation and surveillance. In contrast, binocular infrared night vision lenses feature two eyepieces, providing enhanced depth perception and a more immersive viewing experience, suitable for activities like hunting and tactical operations. Both types effectively amplify available light to enable clear vision in low-light conditions.</p></p>
