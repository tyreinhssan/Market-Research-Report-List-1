<p><p>The miniature camera modules market primarily consists of two imaging technologies: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD imaging is known for its high-quality output and low noise levels, making it suitable for professional applications like medical imaging and high-end photography. In contrast, CMOS imaging offers faster processing speeds and lower power consumption, making it ideal for mobile devices and cost-sensitive applications. Both technologies cater to diverse sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and security.</p></p>
