<p><ul><li>Conventional Seed Potatoes</li><li>Micro Propagation Seed Potatoes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Seed Potatoes market comprises two primary types: Conventional Seed Potatoes and Micro Propagation Seed Potatoes. Conventional seed potatoes are produced through traditional cultivation methods, ensuring genetic stability and disease resistance, suitable for larger farms. In contrast, Micro Propagation Seed Potatoes utilize tissue culture techniques for rapid multiplication, offering high-quality, disease-free planting material. This method allows for year-round production, making it ideal for small-scale farmers and regions with limited growing seasons, enhancing overall potato yield and quality.</p></p>
