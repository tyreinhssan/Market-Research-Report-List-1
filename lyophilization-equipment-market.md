<p><p>The lyophilization equipment market is segmented into manifold lyophilization equipment and rotary lyophilization equipment. Manifold lyophilization equipment utilizes a centralized system to efficiently freeze and dry multiple samples simultaneously, making it suitable for various applications, including pharmaceuticals and biotechnology. Rotary lyophilization equipment, on the other hand, employs a rotating mechanism to enhance heat transfer during sublimation, ensuring uniform drying and improved efficiency. Each type caters to specific needs, enhancing productivity and product quality in different industries.</p></p>
