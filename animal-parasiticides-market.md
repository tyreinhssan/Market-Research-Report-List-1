<p><p>The animal parasiticides market is categorized into three main types: endoparasiticides, ectoparasiticides, and endectocides. Endoparasiticides target internal parasites such as worms and protozoa within the host's body. Ectoparasiticides focus on external parasites, like fleas and ticks, affecting the skin and coat of animals. Endectocides combine the effects of both, treating both internal and external parasite infestations simultaneously. This diverse range of products is crucial for maintaining animal health and preventing disease transmission in livestock and pets.</p></p>
