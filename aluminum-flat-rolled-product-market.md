<p><p>The aluminum flat-rolled product market encompasses three primary types: plates, sheets, and foils. Plates are thick, large panels used in heavy-duty applications like aerospace and automotive industries, providing strength and durability. Sheets are thinner, versatile products suitable for manufacturing, packaging, and construction. Foils are extremely thin, used primarily in food packaging, insulation, and other applications requiring lightweight and barrier properties. Together, these products play a vital role across various sectors, capitalizing on aluminum's lightweight, corrosion resistance, and recyclable nature.</p></p>
