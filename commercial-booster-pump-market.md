<p><strong>The Commercial Booster Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial booster pump market features two main types: single stage and multiple stages. Single stage booster pumps consist of one impeller, making them suitable for low to moderate pressure applications, typically used in smaller systems. In contrast, multiple stage booster pumps have several impellers arranged in series, enabling them to generate higher pressures for larger systems or demanding applications. The choice between these types depends on the specific flow and pressure requirements of the commercial setting.</p></p>
