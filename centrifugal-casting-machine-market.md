<p><p>The centrifugal casting machine market is primarily categorized into vacuum and non-vacuum types. Vacuum centrifugal casting involves creating a vacuum environment to eliminate air and gas contaminants, leading to improved metal quality and reduced porosity in critical applications. In contrast, non-vacuum centrifugal casting operates in open air, making it suitable for less demanding applications where cost-efficiency is a priority. Both types serve different industries and needs, affecting the choice based on desired material properties and production requirements.</p></p>
