<p><ul><li>Bubble</li><li>Electronic</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical humidifiers market comprises various types, including bubble, electronic, and ultrasonic humidifiers. Bubble humidifiers use a water reservoir where air is bubbled through water, adding moisture before delivery to patients. Electronic humidifiers control humidity levels precisely through moisture sensors, ensuring optimal comfort and therapeutic effectiveness. Ultrasonic humidifiers utilize high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist, providing a quiet and efficient method for humidification. Each type serves specific clinical needs, enhancing patient care in respiratory therapy.</p></p>
