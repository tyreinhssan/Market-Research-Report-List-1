<p><p>The fireproof coating market features two primary types: solvent-based and water-based coatings. Solvent-based coatings utilize organic solvents as carriers, offering excellent coverage and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, water-based coatings are eco-friendly, with lower VOC emissions, and are preferred for commercial and residential uses due to their ease of application and drying speed. Both types serve to enhance fire resistance in various materials, ensuring safety and regulatory compliance in building projects.</p></p>
