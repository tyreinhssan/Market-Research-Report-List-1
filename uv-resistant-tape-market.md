<p><p>The UV Resistant Tape market features two main types: single-sided adhesive tapes and double-sided adhesive tapes. Single-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on one side, making them ideal for bonding surfaces where only one side is exposed, providing protection against UV damage. Double-sided adhesive tapes consist of adhesive on both sides, allowing for versatile applications such as mounting, sealing, and laminating. Both types are designed to withstand harsh UV exposure, ensuring durability in outdoor and high-sunlight environments.</p></p>
