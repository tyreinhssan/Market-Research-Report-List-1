<p><strong>The FRP (Fiber-Reinforced Plastic) Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molded Grating</li><li>Pultruded Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FRP (Fiber-Reinforced Plastic) grating market includes two primary types: molded grating and pultruded grating. Molded grating is produced by compressing fiberglass and resin, resulting in a lightweight, corrosion-resistant product suitable for various environments. It typically features a textured surface for improved slip resistance. In contrast, pultruded grating is created through a continuous process, pulling resin-soaked fibers through a die, yielding a stronger and more durable grating ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types offer excellent durability and low maintenance.</p></p>
