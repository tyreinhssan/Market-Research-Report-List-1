<p><p>The endoscopic flushing pump market includes two primary types: gas-powered irrigation pumps and electrical-powered irrigation pumps. Gas-powered irrigation pumps utilize compressed gas for fluid delivery, offering portability and independence from electrical sources, making them suitable for remote locations. In contrast, electrical-powered irrigation pumps are connected to a power source, providing consistent and controlled fluid flow during endoscopic procedures. Both types enhance visualization and cleaning during examinations, catering to different operational needs and preferences in medical settings.</p></p>
