<p><p>The arteriosclerosis detector market comprises various types, primarily focusing on Pulse Wave Velocity (PWV) detection and Pulse Waveform Analyzer detection. PWV detection measures the speed of blood flow in arteries, providing insights into arterial stiffness, which is a key indicator of arteriosclerosis. Pulse Waveform Analyzer detection, on the other hand, analyzes the shape and characteristics of the pulse wave to assess cardiovascular health. Both methods aid in early diagnosis and monitoring of arterial health, facilitating timely management of cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
